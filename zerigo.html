ZerigoDNS for Android

Install QR Code:

ZerigoDNS is an Android app (currently in Alpha) which allows you to browse all of your DNS Zones and Hostnames under your Zerigo.com account. In the future, the app will let you create and edit Zone and Hostname records.

To find your Zerigo DNS API Token, login to the Zerigo website, and under you Zerigo Account, go to the DNS section, click Preferences, make sure you set "API access and Dynamic updates" to "Enabled", then your API Token will be displayed below. You will need this token to login to your account in the app.
